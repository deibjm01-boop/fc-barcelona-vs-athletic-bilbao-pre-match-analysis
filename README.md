# Athletic Club vs FC Barcelona – Pre-Match Tactical Analysis

This project simulates the work of a **performance analyst at Athletic Club** preparing a tactical report ahead of the match against **FC Barcelona**.

The objective of the analysis is to identify key tactical behaviors of FC Barcelona and translate them into **clear and actionable information for both the coaching staff and the players**.

Due to GitHub file size limitations, the presentation and video analysis are hosted externally. All links related to the project can be seen inside external_links.

# Tactical Aspects Analyzed

### Build-up Play

Two main build-up structures were identified in Barcelona’s first phase of possession.

Key elements include:

- Forwards fixing defenders high up the pitch
- Direct play towards the striker
- Midfield movements generating interior spaces
- Constant change of defensive marking references

---

### Organized Attack

Barcelona’s positional attacks rely on **dragging movements and width usage**.

Typical patterns include:

- Striker dragging defenders to create space
- Wingers attacking spaces created by central movements
- Full-backs penetrating wide areas
- Midfielders occupying interior spaces and arriving in the box

---

### Counterattacks

Barcelona’s transitions are characterized by:

- Through balls between defensive lines
- Fast and vertical attacks into the penalty area
- Immediate defensive retreat after losing possession

---

### Defensive Organization

#### High Block

Barcelona frequently applies **high pressing** as a base defensive structure.

Key characteristics:

- Ball recovery attempts in the opponent’s third
- Full-backs jumping to press wide players
- Coordinated pressing triggers

---

#### Mid Block

Barcelona also defends in a **4-2-3-1 mid-block** structure.

Important details:

- Protection of the penalty area
- Reduced defensive width
- Potential space left in the channels

---

### Defensive Transition and Weaknesses

Barcelona’s defensive line often remains **very high**, which creates situations where defenders must cover large distances.

Key observations include:

- Space appearing near the edge of the penalty area
- Defensive gaps behind **Jules Koundé**

These situations may be exploitable through **fast counterattacks and vertical passes**.

---

### Key Player: Lamine Yamal

Lamine Yamal is highlighted as a **key attacking threat**.

Important characteristics:

- Ability to create offensive situations
- Quick shooting after orientation
- Dangerous in wide attacking areas

Defensive considerations include:

- Constant defensive awareness
- Support from wide midfielders
- Potential central defender coverage

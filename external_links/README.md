## Coaching Presentation

Pre-match tactical presentation prepared from the perspective of an **Athletic Club performance analyst**.
View the presentation (PDF):
https://drive.google.com/file/d/104k4uNAz1C8kG0IXp4Hq7z04riJi8ZJH/view

PowerPoint version:
https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ZNRDCu3xsmXipvpw8KeCnzbq1zBfRaTI/edit

The objective of the presentation is to provide the coaching staff with a structured overview of FC Barcelona's tactical behaviour before the match.

The presentation includes the following sections:

### Expected Lineup
Analysis of the **probable starting lineup** and positional structure used by FC Barcelona.

### Build-up Play
Two different **build-up structures** identified in Barcelona's first phase of possession.

### Organized Attack
Patterns observed during **sustained attacking phases**, including positional structure and player movements.

### Counterattack
Analysis of Barcelona's behaviour during **offensive transitions**, focusing on speed, through balls and verticality.

### High Press
**Barcelona´s base defense**. Structure and triggers used by Barcelona when pressing high up the pitch

### Mid Block
Defensive organisation when Barcelona defends in a **mid block**.

### Defensive Transition and Weaknesses
Analysis of Barcelona's **defensive retreat**, identifying potential vulnerabilities.

Particular focus is placed on **Jules Koundé as a potential weak link** in certain defensive situations.

### Key Player: Lamine Yamal
Individual clips highlighting **Lamine Yamal**, identified as a player capable of creating significant offensive danger.
